Photofine Through The Years!

In 2005, amidst a world slowly embracing digital transformation, Photofine was born from a simple yet profound passion – to preserve memories with excellence. Starting as a humble venture with the use of Karizma Albums, our journey began with traditional methods, capturing moments with the heartfelt dedication that only the early days of photography could offer.

Our founder's dream was to create more than just photo albums; it was to craft cherished keepsakes that families and individuals would treasure for years to come. As the years rolled by, Photofine witnessed a remarkable evolution, transforming from its roots in old technology to embracing cutting-edge advancements in the field.

From those early, earnest beginnings to becoming a leading name in the photo printing industry, Photofine has scaled new heights, continually adapting to the ever-evolving landscape of photography. Our commitment to quality and innovation has driven us to blend the nostalgia of the past with the brilliance of the present, ensuring that every print we create is not just a product but a cherished memory in the making.

At Photofine, we are proud of our legacy and excited for the future, as we continue to bring unparalleled craftsmanship and state-of-the-art technology to each and every project.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of photo prints do you offer?

We offer a variety of photo prints including standard photobooks, Megabooks, Minibooks, calendars, and custom covers and bags. Each product is designed to preserve your memories beautifully and with high quality.

How can I place an order?

You can contact us directly via phone or email to discuss your requirements and place an order.

What is the turnaround time for my order?

Our standard turnaround time is typically 2-3 business days. However, this can vary depending on the complexity of your order and current demand. We will provide you with an estimated delivery date upon confirmation of your order.

Can I customize my photo products?

Yes, we offer customization options for all our products. You can personalize photobooks, albums, and covers with your choice of images, text, and design elements. Contact us for detailed customization options.

What should I do if I receive a damaged or incorrect item?

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact us immediately. We will arrange for a replacement or refund based on your preference. Be sure to provide us with your order details and photographs of the issue for a prompt resolution.

Do you offer bulk discounts for large orders?

Yes, we offer bulk discounts for large orders. If you are placing a bulk order for events, corporate gifts, or any other reason, please contact us to discuss your requirements and we’ll provide you with a customized quote.
